Kurt Cobain

  • 1967 - Born in Aberdeen, Washington, USA
  • 1971 - First try at a song at age 4
  • 1976 - Parents split in March, divorce official in July
  • 1979 - Cobain's mother grants full custody of Kurt to his father
  • 1980 - Smokes marijuana for the first time, aged thirteen
  • 1981 - Chooses a used guitar over a bike for his fourteenth birthday
  • 1982 - Records first attempt at a song
  • 1987 - Nirvana's first semi-public gig, Cobain aged 20
  • 1989 - Nirvana's first US tour after only 34 gigs;
    Nirvana's debut album "Bleach" released;
    Nirvana's first European tour "Heavier Than Heaven"
  • 1990 - Cobain first met Courtney Love on Friday, 12 January at Satyricon, a small nightclub in Portland, Oregon;
    Cobain first tries heroin
  • 1991 - "Nevermind" is released
  • 1992 - Cobain marries Love, refuses to stay at Cobain's "dream home";
    Frances Bean Cobain is born on Aug 18
  • 1993 - Cobain first suicide attempt using heroin, Love adminsters more drugs before calling 911;
    Cobain's debilitating stomach pains correctly diagnosed, Cobain realised he can live pain-free without drugs;
    "In Utero" is released;
    Does last interview and photoshoot (unknowingly)
  • 1994 - Cobain overdoses on Rome, recovers twenty hours later, starts rehab
  • Death - Apr 4: Love poses as Cobain's mother, files a missing persons report;
    Cobain was last seen in a restaurant;
    Apr 8: Cobain's body found by electrician
